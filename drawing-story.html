Story

Being a fan of anime, Normal-kun sets out to join his college's anime club. In this club, he meets a girl named Teresa. I plan on most characters to have an opposite-gender counterpart.

Main Characters

Normal-kun (M/20)

Normal-kun is your average protagonist. He is 20 years old and loves anime and video games. I'll try to make him unique by resembling me.

Teresa (F/18)

Teresa is a pink haired girl with twintails. She loves being cute, is somewhat of an otaku, and is good friends with Xandra. She doesn't mind being cute and small.

Xandra (F/21)

Xandra is a stage magician who loves performing magic tricks.

Ruby (F/23)

Ruby is a very little girl, but she's actually a full-time elementary school teacher. She became a hikikomori at age 8, but decided to become a teacher recently. One of her pet peeves is being mistaken as a 7 or 8 year old, although she does use her mistaken age to get cheaper admission. Her catchphrase is "I'm 23, dang it!"

Unnamed maid (F/20)

She is a maid working in a mansion. She has light blue hair and is the calm, mature type.

Supporting Characters

Normal-kun's brother (M/17)

Normal-kun's younger brother who is not into anime.

Elizabeth (F/15)

Normal-kun's blonde older little sister. After attending a magic show by Xandra, she wanted to get into magic.

Nicole (F/11)

Normal-kun's blonde younger little sister. After attending a magic show by Xandra, she wanted to get into magic.

Unnamed boy (M/12)

A boy that Nicole hates for some strange reason.

Olivia (F/7) and Elise (F/3)

Olivia is one of Ruby's students and lives in a rich mansion. Her younger sister, Elise, loves Olivia and big hats. They love playing pretend and dress up.

Unnamed boy (M/8)

A classmate of Olivia who is one of her best friends.

Ava (F/18)

Ava is Teresa's British alter-ego who works as a maid working in Olivia's mansion.

Unnamed teacher A (M/27)

A young man who Ruby has a crush on

Unnamed teacher B (F/26)

An unmarried woman who is very helpful to Ruby

Robert (M/40)

Ruby's British cousin and has produced a lot of music starting from the 90s.
