Make Chiptunes
  1. You're doing this for the sake of creativity.
  2. Your second project should be a chiptune song or remix lasting around 2 minutes.
  3. I don't care if your first song sounds like crap. Just upload it! Seek for constructive criticism!
  4. Search for lessons on how to make chiptune, like EliteFerrex.
  5. Try learning all the things that you don't understand. What are they, and why are they used?
  6. By learning chiptune music, you'll learn the basics of electronic music production for CHEAP.
  7. Watch this if you want to learn how to use FL Studio.
