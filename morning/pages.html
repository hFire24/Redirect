How to use morning pages

  1. Use morning pages to write down any thoughts you may have upon waking up.
  2. Some thoughts to write down include
    • What your dreams were
    • What you want to search for along with the thoughts that come with it
    • What your objectives of the day are
  3. Why write morning pages? To prevent wasted mornings.
  4. It doesn't matter how riculous your thoughts are. If you think about it, write it.
  5. Some interesting patterns should emerge from your morning pages.
  6. Once you're done writing in your morning pages, meditate to give your mornings a boost.
  7. Or perhaps, you may listen to music to control your thoughts.
