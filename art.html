Start drawing
  1. You're doing this for the sake of creativity.
  2. Draw with anything you prefer, whether it be a mouse, a sketchpad, or an iPad.
  3. Best advice for now is to draw anything you're thinking of.
  4. If you can't think of anything, click here.
  5. Don't compare your sketches with other people except yourself.
  6. Use references! They are incredibly helpuful.
  7. Be sure you draw every day.
  8. You can draw more than one sketch per day.
  9. In the future, you should get professional anime drawing lessons or an anime art tutor.
  10. Once drawing becomes a habit, get a drawing tablet.
